# Natural Phrasing Preservation Skill

## Purpose
Transform English expressions into natural French equivalents, avoiding literal translations that sound awkward.

## Avoir Expressions (Have → Be)

Many English "to be" expressions use "avoir" (to have) in French:

| English | French | Literal (WRONG) |
|---------|--------|-----------------|
| I am hungry | J'**ai** faim | ~~Je suis faim~~ |
| I am thirsty | J'**ai** soif | ~~Je suis soif~~ |
| I am cold | J'**ai** froid | ~~Je suis froid~~ |
| I am hot | J'**ai** chaud | ~~Je suis chaud~~ |
| I am afraid | J'**ai** peur | ~~Je suis peur~~ |
| I am right | J'**ai** raison | ~~Je suis correct~~ |
| I am wrong | J'**ai** tort | ~~Je suis wrong~~ |
| I am X years old | J'**ai** X ans | ~~Je suis X ans~~ |
| I am sleepy | J'**ai** sommeil | ~~Je suis fatigué~~ |
| I am lucky | J'**ai** de la chance | ~~Je suis chanceux~~ |

## Reversed Subject Constructions

### "To Miss" Pattern
English "I miss you" → French "Tu me manques" (You are missing to me)

| English | French | Structure |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| I miss you | Tu me manques | You-to me-miss |
| She misses him | Il lui manque | He-to her-misses |
| We miss Paris | Paris nous manque | Paris-to us-misses |

### "To Like/Love" with People
- "I like her" → "Elle me plaît" (She pleases me) - romantic
- "I like her" → "Je l'aime bien" (casual liking)

## Reflexive Verbs

Many actions are reflexive in French but not in English:

| English | French |
|---------|--------|
| I wake up | Je **me** réveille |
| I get dressed | Je **m'**habille |
| I remember | Je **me** souviens |
| I wash | Je **me** lave |
| I sit down | Je **m'**assieds |
| I wonder | Je **me** demande |
| I hurry | Je **me** dépêche |

## Time Expressions

| English | French | Note |
|---------|--------|------|
| It's 3 o'clock | Il est trois heures | Not "C'est" |
| In the morning | Le matin | With definite article |
| On Monday | Lundi | No preposition |
| Every day | Tous les jours | "All the days" |

## Idiomatic Differences

| English | French (Natural) | Literal (AVOID) |
|---------|------------------|-----------------|
| It's raining | Il pleut | ~~C'est pleuvant~~ |
| What's your name? | Comment tu t'appelles? | ~~Quel est ton nom?~~ |
| I don't care | Je m'en fiche | ~~Je ne care pas~~ |
| It doesn't matter | Ça ne fait rien | ~~Ça n'a pas d'importance~~ |
| I feel like | J'ai envie de | ~~Je me sens comme~~ |

## Word Order Adjustments

### Adjective Placement (BANGS)
Adjectives for Beauty, Age, Number, Goodness, Size come BEFORE the noun:

- "A beautiful woman" → "Une **belle** femme"
- "An old house" → "Une **vieille** maison"
- "A big dog" → "Un **gros** chien"

### Other Adjectives Follow Noun
- "A red car" → "Une voiture **rouge**"
- "A French book" → "Un livre **français**"

## Quality Criteria
- No literal translations of idiomatic expressions
- Correct avoir/être selection
- Proper reflexive verb usage
- Natural French word order
- Native-speaker phrasing

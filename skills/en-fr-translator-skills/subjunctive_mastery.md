# Subjunctive Mastery Skill

## Purpose
Master the French subjunctive mood which is required in contexts where English uses indicative.

## Trigger Conditions

The subjunctive (subjonctif) is required after:

### 1. Verbs of Desire/Wish
- vouloir que (to want that)
- souhaiter que (to wish that)
- désirer que (to desire that)
- aimer que (to like that)

**Examples:**
- "I want him to come" → "Je veux qu'il **vienne**" (not "vient")
- "She wishes you were here" → "Elle souhaite que tu **sois** ici"

### 2. Verbs of Emotion
- être content que (to be happy that)
- être triste que (to be sad that)
- avoir peur que (to be afraid that)
- regretter que (to regret that)

**Examples:**
- "I'm happy you're here" → "Je suis content que tu **sois** là"
- "She's afraid he'll leave" → "Elle a peur qu'il **parte**"

### 3. Verbs of Doubt/Uncertainty
- douter que (to doubt that)
- ne pas penser que (to not think that)
- ne pas croire que (to not believe that)

**Examples:**
- "I doubt he knows" → "Je doute qu'il **sache**"
- "I don't think she's coming" → "Je ne pense pas qu'elle **vienne**"

### 4. Impersonal Expressions
- il faut que (it is necessary that)
- il est important que (it is important that)
- il est possible que (it is possible that)

**Examples:**
- "It's necessary that you go" → "Il faut que tu **ailles**"
- "It's important that she knows" → "Il est important qu'elle **sache**"

### 5. Conjunctions
- pour que (so that)
- avant que (before)
- bien que (although)
- jusqu'à ce que (until)

**Examples:**
- "Before he leaves" → "Avant qu'il **parte**"
- "Although she's tired" → "Bien qu'elle **soit** fatiguée"

## Common Subjunctive Forms

| Infinitive | Subjunctive (je) | Subjunctive (il/elle) |
|------------|------------------|----------------------|
| être | sois | soit |
| avoir | aie | ait |
| aller | aille | aille |
| faire | fasse | fasse |
| pouvoir | puisse | puisse |
| savoir | sache | sache |
| venir | vienne | vienne |
| prendre | prenne | prenne |

## Decision Process

1. Identify the main verb in English
2. Check if it triggers subjunctive (desire, emotion, doubt, necessity)
3. If yes, use "que" + subjunctive verb form
4. Conjugate the dependent verb in subjunctive mood

## Quality Criteria
- All subjunctive triggers correctly identified
- Proper subjunctive conjugation applied
- Natural French sentence structure maintained
